Answers to the questions we get asked the most about.

Now we want to help independent businesses like yourself (and us) make the most of their limited resources and not waste time and money.

If you want more details contact us on: alan@bookmi.place. We're friendly and we want to speak to you 😀.

How much does it cost?

4% (of each booking that we handle for you).

Break it down...

1.5%
of that is the card handler fee (so you'd have to pay that anyway if you took card payments) and the remaining 2.5% goes to us.

Just 2.5%?!?
Yes, we know. We're nice guys. For 2.5% we handle all your bookings, deal with all your payment processing, emails, chasing of late payments, organising customer data, live updating of trip numbers... so much... for so little...

Where does the money go?

We use the card handling service Stripe to process your customer's payment (they're the ones charging the 1.5% :). Once payment is succesful, it goes into a holding account. This is our business account in the UK. Log in to your secure account to see how much money your customers have paid you at any time.

The day after the trip commences, all your money is automatically transfered into your nominated bank account. The reason the money is held in the holding acccount is to allow us to handle refunds on your behalf. Saving you the hassle of dealing with bank transfers - yet again! 👏

Is there a minimum contract? / Am I tied into a subscription?

No.

We're nice guys and we don't want to tie you up if you don't want to be with us.

Come and go as you please. If you have a couple of quiet months, then there's no cost. We're trying to help you save time and make money, not force you to pay for something you don't want.
